Welcome To M.P. Memorial School

M.P. Memorial School & Hostel was founded by M.P. Memorial Educational Society
(Regd. Under societies act) in the year 1996 with the soul motto to provide cheap and good
education to the students and upliftment of society and the area. The school has been
emphasizing on providing quality education which would be in line with the national education policy -1986.
Since its beginning the school has made quantum jumps in the academics, co-curricular activities, games and sports.
